The QAU Alumni Association North America launches its membership campaign and would like to invite you to become its inaugural members by filling out the following form.

Eligibillity

The membership is open to everyone who has an affiliation with QAU in any of the following capacities:

  • Received a degree, diploma, or certificate from the University in a certified program of one year or more (regular member)

  • Any past or present academic staff member of the University (associated member)

  • Any person who has received an honorary degree from the University (honorary member)

Benifits

Benefits for members:

  • Access to the QAU-NA Alumni directory
  • Eligible to vote and be part of the decision-making process
  • Eligible to run for QAU-NA office positions
  • Priority invitation to QAU-NA events
  • Members discount on QAU-NA events
  • Group rate on QAU-NA participated events and products

Annual Membership Fee

  • USD$ 25 for members residing in the U.S.A.
  • CAD$ 35 for memmbers residing in Canada
