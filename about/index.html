Some of the former senior Quadians, now based in North America, have been discussing the need to establish QAU Alumni in North America for quite some time. Since 2021 few former Quadians based in North America met multiple times in Toronto to formalize the alumni association.

On 8th October 2022, the first event of QAU Alumni North America was launched in Oakville, Ontario, where about 100 Quadians participated. After the pandemic, it was the first large-scale gathering of Quadians in North America.

Quadians from Canada included Toronto, Scarborough, Brampton, Mississauga, Milton, Oakville, Vaughan, Markham, Ottawa, and Vancouver.

Quadians from the USA included Detroit, Chicago, New Jersey, Virginia, and virtually from Dallas.

This was a diverse gathering of Quaidians ranging from 1973 to 2013 from several departments, including Administrative Sciences (MBA, MPA), Chemistry, Computer Science, DSS, Economics, Electronics, Geophysics, History, International Relations, Mathematics, and Pakistan Studies.

At the event, the interim Board of Directors was announced to be in place for the next two years to get the Association on its feet. Subsequently, on 12 October 2022, the QAU Alumni Association of North America - QAU-AANA was formally registered with Corporation Canada.
